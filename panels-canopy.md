# Canopy

[TOC]

## Description

The canopy is the primary transparent enclosure on N657CZ. It is a blown acrylic panel, vacuum-formed to the compound curve of the cockpit opening. The canopy provides weather protection, aerodynamic fairing, and occupant visibility forward and to the sides. It is located above and aft of the instrument panel.

## Inspection

Inspect the canopy at each condition inspection.

**Acrylic surface:**

- **Crazing**: Look for a network of fine surface cracks. Crazing results from stress, solvent contact, or heat. Any crazing warrants evaluation for replacement. (AC 43.13, ch03_p020)
- **Cracks**: At the first sign of crack development, stop-drill the crack ends with a #30 (1/8 in.) drill to prevent further propagation. Schedule for repair or replacement. (AC 43.13, ch03_p024, ch03_p040)
- **Scratches**: Deep scratches that impair pilot visibility require repair or replacement. Minor surface scratches may be polished out per the procedure in AC 43.13, ch03_p040.
- **Optical clarity**: Check for hazing, yellowing, or optical distortion.

> **NOTE:** Replace rather than repair extensively damaged transparent plastic whenever possible. Repairs must not be located in the pilot's line of vision. (AC 43.13, ch03_p024)
