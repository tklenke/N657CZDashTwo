Scheduled servicing tasks and life-limited items for N657CZ. Complete all applicable tasks at or before their due date.

## Each Condition Inspection

- **Engine oil and oil filter change** — See [[Oil System|powerplant-oil-system]] in Section 14.
- **Engine oil analysis sample** — See [[Oil System|powerplant-oil-system]] in Section 14.
- **Cylinder compression and borescope inspection** — See [[Engine|powerplant-engine]] in Section 14.
- **Spark plug cleaning, inspection, and gapping** — See [[Ignition System|powerplant-ignition-system]] in Section 14.
- **Air filter inspection** — See [[Engine|powerplant-engine]] in Section 14.
- **Crankcase vent PCV valve cleaning** — See [[Oil System|powerplant-oil-system]] in Section 14. Also due every 100 hours — see [Every 100 Hours](#every-100-hours).
- **Brake fluid (MIL-H-5606A) visual check** — See [[Brake System|landing-gear-brake-system]] in Section 13. Full flush due on calendar interval — see [Life-Limited Items](#life-limited-items).
- **ELT battery check and functional test** — Per 14 CFR 91.207(c) and (d). See [[Emergency Locator Transmitter (ELT)|emergency-elt]] in Section 11.

## Every 100 Hours (or Each CI, Whichever Comes First)

- **Crankcase vent PCV valve cleaning** — See [[Oil System|powerplant-oil-system]] in Section 14. Also due at each condition inspection — see [Each Condition Inspection](#each-condition-inspection).
- **Fuel injector restrictor cleaning** — First clean at 500 hours; then every 100 hours or each CI thereafter. See [[Pumps and Valves|fuel-pumps-and-valves]] in Section 15. Also see [Every 500 Hours](#every-500-hours).
