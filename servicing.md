# Servicing

::: draft
**DRAFT** — Not yet reviewed.
:::

The following tasks are performed on a scheduled basis. Intervals are calendar- or hour-based as noted. Complete all applicable tasks at or before their due date.

---

## Engine Oil and Oil Filter Change

**Interval:** Annual, or per oil analysis results
**Procedure:** See [[Oil System|powerplant-oil-system]] in Section 14.

## Engine Oil Analysis Sample

**Interval:** Annual
**Procedure:** See [[Oil System|powerplant-oil-system]] in Section 14.

## Cylinder Compression and Borescope Inspection

**Interval:** Annual (at each condition inspection)
**Procedure:** See [[Engine|powerplant-engine]] in Section 14.

## Spark Plug Cleaning, Inspection, and Gapping

**Interval:** Annual (at each condition inspection)
**Procedure:** See [[Ignition System|powerplant-ignition-system]] in Section 14.

## Fuel Injector Restrictor Cleaning

**Interval:** At 500 hours, then every 100 hours or each condition inspection thereafter
**Procedure:** See [[Pumps and Valves|fuel-pumps-and-valves]] in Section 15.

## Crankcase Vent PCV Valve Cleaning

**Interval:** Every 100 hours or each condition inspection
**Procedure:** See [[Oil System|powerplant-oil-system]] in Section 14.

## Air Filter Inspection

**Interval:** Annual (at each condition inspection)
**Procedure:** See [[Engine|powerplant-engine]] in Section 14.

## Nose Wheel Bearing Regreasing

**Interval:** 300 hours or at tire replacement (@@TOM: verify interval and source document)
**Procedure:** See [[Nose Gear|landing-gear-nose]] in Section 13.

## Main Wheel Bearing Regreasing

**Interval:** 300 hours or at tire replacement (@@TOM: verify interval and source document)
**Procedure:** See [[Main Landing Gear|landing-gear-main]] in Section 13.

## Brake Fluid Check

**Interval:** Annual visual check
**Procedure:** See [[Brake System|landing-gear-brake-system]] in Section 13.

## ELT Battery Check and Functional Test

**Interval:** Per 14 CFR 91.207(c) and (d)
**Procedure:** See [[Emergency Locator Transmitter (ELT)|emergency-elt]] in Section 11.

## Oxygen System Inspection and Cylinder Recertification Check

**Interval:** Annual inspection; hydrostatic test every 5 years (standard-weight DOT 3AA 1800) or 3 years (lightweight DOT 3HT 1850) (AC 43.13, ch09_p051)
**Procedure:** See [[Oxygen System|cabin-oxygen-system]] in Section 10.
