## Overview

The annual condition inspection satisfies the requirement of 14 CFR Part 91.409(e) for experimental amateur-built aircraft. It is conducted by the owner or by any appropriately rated A&P mechanic. The inspection follows the checklist structure established by Marc J. Zeitlin A&P #3052594, adapted and filtered to the N657CZ configuration (COZY Mark IV with Roncz canard, Lycoming IO-360, Airflow Performance fuel injection, Lightspeed electronic ignition, Garmin G3X avionics).

## Tools and Materials Required

Refer to the consumables list for the current annual in the aircraft logbook. Typical consumables include:

- Engine oil: Aeroshell 15W-50, Phillips XC 20W-50, Aeroshell W100+, or equivalent
- Oil filter: CH48108-1 or equivalent
- Oil analysis sample kit (Aviation Oil Analysis, Blackstone, or equivalent)
- Wheel bearing grease: synthetic wheel bearing grease (Mobil 1 or equivalent)
- Spark plugs (upper and lower sets), as required
- Air filter / recharge kit
- Brake fluid: MIL-H-5606A — **must match fluid already installed** — MIL fluids are mutually compatible; DOT fluids are not compatible with MIL fluids and must not be mixed

## Safety Notes

Follow all applicable safety precautions. See Section 2 for general hazards.

Work on the fuel and ignition systems with the master switch off and fuel valve in the OFF position. Confirm the fuel selector is OFF before removing any fuel-system components.

## Prerequisites

- Aircraft clean per 14 CFR Part 43, Appendix D(a)
- Aircraft on jacks (required for Fuselage Bottom section)
- All access panels, fairings, and covers available for removal
- Engine logbook, airframe logbook, and propeller logbook on hand

## Inspection Checklist

### Documents and Placards

- **Paperwork envelope** — Check condition and visibility per 14 CFR 91.203(b)
- **Airworthiness certificate, registration, operating limitations** — Verify all are current and in order
- **Weight and balance / equipment list** — Check; update if necessary
- **Airframe, engine, and propeller logbooks** — Check condition and compliance with 14 CFR 91.417
- **AD compliance list** — Review; comply as deemed necessary
- **Service bulletin / plans changes list** — Review; comply as deemed necessary
- **Dataplate** — Check condition and compliance with 14 CFR 45.13
- **"Experimental" placard** — Check condition and compliance with 14 CFR 45.23(b)
- **Instrument and switch labeling** — Check condition
- **Passenger warning placard** — Check condition and compliance with operating limitations
- **Registration markings** — Check N-numbers for compliance with 14 CFR 45.21
